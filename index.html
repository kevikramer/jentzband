Who We Are

Based in Butler, PA, The Jentz brings the best of classic rock to stages across the Greater Pittsburgh area. Known for our tight rhythm section, searing guitar solos, and powerful vocals, we deliver a high-energy experience that keeps crowds on their feet.

Whether we're playing at local favorites like the Helltown Taproom or rocking outdoor festivals, our setlist is packed with the hits you know and love. We don't just play music; we put on a show.
